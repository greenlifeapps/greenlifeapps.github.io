Overview

A quick overview of how to use the app

  • 1
    Create a new Account, it's free!

    It takes a few minutes. Enter basic information about your business, and get started!

    Add a service that you office. Enter more services via settings screen.

  • 2
    Create your first Job

    Enter job information, select the services for this job!

    Enter a new account for this job, or select an existing account

  • 3
    Confirm the job!

    See the job on the calendar, as well as on a map. Go to Jobs screen to see the list of all your jobs.

    Go to Accounts screen to see all your accounts. Manage your clients, and create new jobs for them.
